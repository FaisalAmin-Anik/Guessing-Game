Guessing Number Game

Rule:

  • Player 1 will choose a number between 0-10 and Player 2 will try to guess it.
  • Player 2 has 3 chances to guess the number correctly.
  • If Player 2 guesses the number correctly, they win. If they run out of chances, Player 1 wins.
